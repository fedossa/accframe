Below, you find a table that shows the multipliers that Participant B can choose from and their costs to Participant B. The multiplier that Participant B chooses determines your payoff. Your payoff will be calculated as (your endowment - number of points sent to Participant B) times the multiplier selected by Participant B. The payoff of Participant B will be calculated as the number of points you sent to Participant B times the cost of the multiplier selected by Participant B.

  • Payoff Participant B = number of points you sent to Participant B * cost of multiplier selected by Participant B
