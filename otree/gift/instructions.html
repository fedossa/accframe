This is an experiment on decision-making. You have been randomly and anonymously paired with another participant. One of you will be selected at random to be participant A; the other will be participant B. You will learn whether you are participant A or B prior to making any decision.

{{ if C.NUM_ROUNDS == 1 }} This experiment will be played for only 1 round. First, {{ else }} This experiment will be played over {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds. Throughout the experiment, you will remain in the same role. Each round, {{ endif }} participant A has an initial wealth of 100 and has to choose an amount that she or he will send to participant B. This amount can be any integer between 0 and {{ C.WAGE_MAX }}. Next, participant B has to decide on an effort, inducing an effort cost. This effort cost will reduce her or his payoff so that the payoff of participant B will be the amount sent by participant A minus the effort cost. Participant B cannot choose an effort cost that is higher than the amount received from participant A. She or he can choose between 10 different effort costs from 0 to 18. Each cost level is linked to an effort multiplier between 0.1 and 1.0 as detailed by the table below.

{{ include_sibling 'multiplier_table.html' }}

The effort multiplier will determine the payoff of participant A. This payoff is calculated as the product of the effort multiplier with the difference of participant A's initial wealth of 100 minus the amount sent to participant B.

  • Payoff A = Effort Multiplier * (100 - Amount sent by A)
  • Payoff B = Amount sent by A - Effort Cost

So, for example, if participant A chooses to send an amount 50 to participant B and participant B chooses an effort cost of 10, the payoff to participant B will be 50 - 10 = 40 and the payoff to participant A will be 0.7 * (100 - 50) = 35.

{{ if C.NUM_ROUNDS == 1 }} The objective of the game is to maximize your payoff. {{ else }} The payoffs that both players receive in each round increase their private wealth. The objective of the game is to maximize your private wealth over the duration of the game. {{ endif }}
