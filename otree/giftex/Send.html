{{ if C.NUM_ROUNDS > 1 }} As in every round, you {{ else }} You {{ endif }} received an initial endowment of 100 points and have to decide on the number of points that you will send to Participant B. This amount can be any integer between 0 and {{ C.WAGE_MAX }} points. After observing the number of points they received, Participant B will select a multiplier. Multipliers are costly for Participant B. Below, you find a table that shows the multipliers that Participant B can choose from and their costs to Participant B. The multiplier that Participant B chooses determines your payoff. Your payoff will be calculated as (your endowment - number of points sent to Participant B) times the multiplier selected by Participant B. The payoff of Participant B will be calculated as the number of points you sent to Participant B minus the cost of the multiplier selected by Participant B. In summary:

  • Your payoff = (100 - number of points you send to Participant B) * multiplier selected by Participant B
  • Payoff Participant B = number of points you send to Participant B * cost of multiplier selected by Participant B
