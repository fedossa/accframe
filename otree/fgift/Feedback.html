The payoffs of the participants for the setting below are calculated as follows:

  • Each round, the initial endowment of the firm is 100 points. The manager has an initial endowment of zero.
  • The firm rewarded the manager a wage of 10 points, reducing its endowment from 100 to 90 points.
  • The manager decided for an effort level of 0.3.
  • Following the table below this implies a cost of 2 points.
  • The manager's payoff is 10 points (the wage received) - 2 points (the effort cost) = 8 points.
  • The firm's payoff is the endowment after sending the amount (100 - 10) = 90 points times the effort level chosen by the manager (0.3) = 27 points.
