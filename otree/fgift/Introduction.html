This is an experiment on managerial compensation and managerial effort. You have been randomly and anonymously paired with another participant. One of you will represent the role of a supervisory board deciding on managerial compensation. The other will be the manager deciding on managerial effort. You will learn whether you are representing the supervisory board or the manager prior to taking any decision.

{{ if C.NUM_ROUNDS == 1 }} This experiment will be played for only 1 round. First, {{ else }} This experiment will be played over {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds. Throughout the experiment, you will remain in the same role. Each round, {{ endif }} the supervisory board has to decide on the compensation that it will pay to the manager. This amount can be any integer between 0 and the total value of the firm ({{ C.WAGE_MAX }}). Next, the manager has to decide on her or his effort level. She or he can choose between 10 different effort levels. Each effort level will be causing an effort cost to the manager ranging from 0 to 18 and an effort multiplier affecting firm value between 0.1 and 1 in the way that a higher effort cost implies a higher effort multiple. The link is as follows:

{{ include_sibling 'multiplier_table.html' }}

The payoff of the manager will be her or his compensation minus minus the effort cost. The payoff of the supervisory board will be based on the difference of the firm value of 100 and the managerial compensation. This difference will be multiplied by the effort multiplier that corresponds to the effort cost that the manager is willing to incur in order to to increase firm value. If this value is not an integer it will be rounded to yield the payoff to the supervisory board.

So, for example, if the supervisory board chooses a compensation of 50 and the manager chooses an effort cost of 10, the payoff to the manager will be will be 50 - 10 = 40 and the payoff to the supervisory board will be (100 - 50) * 0.7 = 35.

{{ if C.NUM_ROUNDS == 1 }} The objective of the game is to maximize your payoff. {{ else }} The payoffs that both players receive in each round increase their private wealth. The objective of the game is to maximize your private wealth over the duration of the game. {{ endif }}
